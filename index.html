About Me

Hello! My name is Stein Duray, a 19-year-old web developer and designer based in the vibrant city of Amsterdam. I have been passionately coding and honing my skills for the past 3 years, with a strong focus on creating visually appealing and user-friendly websites. As a tech enthusiast, I love keeping up with the latest trends and incorporating new technologies into my projects. My diverse work experience includes bartending, sales, and design, which has given me a unique perspective on various industries and the ability to adapt to different environments. My ultimate dream is to travel the world while doing what I love: coding and designing websites that make a difference. I'm excited to take on new challenges and continuously grow both personally and professionally. Let's collaborate and create something amazing together!
