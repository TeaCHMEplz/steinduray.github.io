Welcome! My name is Stein Duray, a 19-year-old web developer born and raised in

the vibrant city center of Amsterdam. Growing up, computers have always played a significant role in my life, providing a logical and creative outlet. Despite my passion for technology, I found it challenging to fit into the traditional school system due to its lack of creativity and autonomy. At the age of 18, I made the bold decision to discontinue my formal education and pursue my passion for coding independently. Over the past three years, I have honed my skills in web development, focusing on creating visually appealing and user-friendly websites. My diverse work experience, ranging from snowboarding instructor to door-to-door sales, has equipped me with a unique perspective and the ability to adapt to various industries. As a tech enthusiast, I strive to stay current with the latest trends and incorporate cutting-edge technologies, such as AI, into my projects. My ultimate dream is to travel the world while doing what I love: coding and designing websites that make a difference. Driven by my curiosity and love for problem-solving, I am eager to take on new challenges and grow both personally and professionally. Let's collaborate and create something amazing together!
